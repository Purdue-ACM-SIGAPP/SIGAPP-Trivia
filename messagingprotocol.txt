Joining a game:
	Phone sends:
		"roomid
		 username
		"
	server replies:
		"accepted"
		OR
		"gtfo"

Starting a new question:
	videowall sends:
		"starting new question"
	Phones can start sending responses.

End of question:
	videowall sends:
		"question end
		 correct answer: (a, b, c, or d)
		 Leaderboard:
		 (username) top-score
		 (username) second-score
		 (username) third-score
		 ... ALL scores sorted ...
		 End leaderboard"

Answering a question:
	phone sends:
		"(username) (a, b, c, or d)"
	videowall replies:
		"(username) answer recieved"
