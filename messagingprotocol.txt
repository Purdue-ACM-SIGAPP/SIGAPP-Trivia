Joining a game:
	Phone sends:
		"join (username)"
	videowall replies:
		"(username) accepted"
		OR
		"(username) gtfo"

Starting a new question:
	videowall sends:
		"starting new question (currenttime)"
	phones respone:
		"(username) is active"
	Phones can start sending responses.

End of question:
	videowall sends:
		"question end. correct answer (answer)"

Answering a question:
	phone sends:
		"(username) (answer a, b, c, or d)"
	videowall replies:
		"(username) answer recieved"
